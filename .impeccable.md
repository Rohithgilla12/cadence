# Cadence — Impeccable Design Context

Synthesized from the PRD (private — `~/cadence-private-docs/PRD.md` locally), `docs/DESIGN_SYSTEM.md`, and `CLAUDE.md`. This document condenses the design-relevant signal so future `/impeccable` invocations don't need to re-derive it.

## Users

Mid-20s to mid-40s **runners who think** — specifically the author's circle of MAF-trained aerobic-base runners. iPhone-first (launch audience is 10–20 friends, mostly in India + a few abroad). They open Cadence before a morning run, during cool-down, or in bed before sleep. Already on Strava but tired of the social pressure that distorts training toward visible PRs.

Expanded audience over time: meditation/journaling adopters disillusioned with Streaks and Notion. **Universal mechanics, runner-specific first community.**

See PRD §4 for the full audience treatment.

## Brand Personality

**Three words: quiet, honest, considered.**

- **Quiet** — Headspace-adjacent, not Duolingo-adjacent. Saturated color is an accent, not a wash.
- **Honest** — when the analytics engine finds no qualifying pattern, the UI says "Cadence is listening." We never invent insights to perform value.
- **Considered** — every word, every animation, every shadow has been thought through. The aesthetic is the product's voice (DS §1).

The voice guide is PRD §20. Forbidden words: "streak" as a verb, "failed", "goal", "productivity", "crush", "awesome", "don't worry", "compete". Allowed words: practice, rhythm, notice, quiet, coming back, lever, circle, pact, flower.

## Aesthetic Direction

**Earth tones on paper.** Cream backgrounds (`#F4F3ED`), white cards with hairline borders, moss as the only saturated accent (`#4A5A40`). Sand for grace days, clay for "over-zone" warmth. Dust tones (blue/pink/cream/lilac) for avatars only, deterministically derived from user names.

**Typography is the brand.** Iowan Old Style serif for headlines, system-ui for body. The serif/sans contrast is the signature. Italics belong in the serif voice; numbers in stat values are serif.

**Light mode only in v1.** The splash screen is the only dark surface (`#2C3528` moss-ink). Dark mode is v2 territory.

**Quiet motion.** Reanimated 3 worklets only. Easings curve `cubic-bezier(0.2, 0, 0.13, 1)`. Durations cap at 320ms. Recovery card "breathing" loop is the showcase moment — subtle 1.0→1.03 scale over 4 seconds via Skia.

References that capture the right feel: Headspace, Day One journal, Linear (mid-2024, before the gradient pivot), Notion's earlier marketing site, a museum exhibit caption printed on uncoated stock.

Anti-references: Duolingo (mascot energy), Strava (social pressure feed), Habitica (gamification), Whoop (clinical/anxious), any "AI dashboard" with cyan-on-dark and gradient hero numbers, anything that says "crush your goals."

## Design Principles

The five aesthetic commitments from DS §1, condensed:

1. **Quiet, not loud.** Most of the screen is paper, cream, and soft moss. Accents are rare and earn their loudness.
2. **Serif headlines, sans body.** Headlines feel like a journal entry. Body feels like a well-designed interface.
3. **Generous whitespace.** Cards have padding. Sections have margins. Density is the enemy.
4. **No hard edges, no hard contrasts.** Border radii are present everywhere. Borders are 0.5px hairlines.
5. **Earth tones, not pastels.** Palette draws from moss, sand, clay, and paper.

## Hard rules (mirroring DS §12)

- **No red.** Sand for gentle warnings, clay for over-zone, ink-2 for muted destructive actions.
- **No emoji as UI.** Mood is dots, not 😢😊. Reactions are a single flower icon.
- **No gradient text. No glassmorphism. No neumorphism. No drop shadows on text.**
- **No badges with numbers > 99.**
- **No PRO labels in v1.** Monetization is deferred; don't pre-design paywall UX.
- **No progress bars that fill to celebration.** Cadence doesn't celebrate completion the Duolingo way.
- **No new colors, fonts, icons** without updating `docs/DESIGN_SYSTEM.md` first.

## Tension with the impeccable defaults

The impeccable skill's "absolute bans" include side-stripe borders (`border-left:` > 1px) as a top-three AI design tell. **Cadence's existing `InsightCard` deliberately uses `border-l-2 border-l-moss-light`** — this is a one-off intentional accent for "pattern noticed" cards in §8, not a habit. The pattern is locked into the design system. If we ever revisit it, do so by amending `DESIGN_SYSTEM.md` first.

The impeccable rules against monoculture fonts are moot here — Cadence uses Iowan Old Style + system-ui, neither of which is in the rejection list. Good.

## When applying impeccable craft

Impeccable should be invoked for:
- Building new surfaces that need a memorable moment (recovery moment, splash, hero on landing page)
- Adding motion to existing screens via Reanimated 3 / Skia
- Refining typography rhythm on text-heavy screens (Reflect, habit detail)
- Designing empty states that teach the interface in Cadence's voice

It should NOT be invoked to:
- Override `DESIGN_SYSTEM.md` tokens (always check there first)
- Introduce new colors, fonts, or radii (those updates start in the design system file)
- Add gamification, celebration, or any of the explicit non-goals in PRD §5
